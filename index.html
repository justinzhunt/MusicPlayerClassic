Music Player Classic

  • Music Player Classic is an app that plays the local music that you already have in the Apple Music app. By mirroring the stock music app's library, rather than creating its own, you preserve the ability to sync your music with your device, while still allowing for a clean and timeless design.

  • Full metadata editing of all the usual music file properties.

  • Album artwork window with player controls that fade away when the mouse leaves the window.

  • Mini Player that is resizeable and has all the normal player controls, as well as a display view that scrolls the song info whenever it is too long to be seen at once.
